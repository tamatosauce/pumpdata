PoolStake by Peet DeFi
Intro to Mulit-blockchain Staking Pools

What are staking pools?
Staking Pools are pools of assets held in smart contract and used for earning collective returns through farming, lending, or staking. By pooling assets network fees are shared and the cost per user is drastically decreased., reducing fees and helping users keep more of what is earned.

How are PTE/PSK used in staking pools?
PTE/PSK added to staking pools represent each users share of that pool and in turn their share of that pool's returns. Let's say you contribute 10% of the tokens in a pool, you are entitled to 10% of that pools return. Each pool is backed by the native asset of the chain it represents, that native asset is then used for lending/farming the top returns available. For example: Staking PTE/PSK in an Eth network pool will pay out any Eth based asset (Eth, Usdt, Dai, etc). Staking tokens in a Neo network pool will pay out Neo assets. Uniquely PSK can be staked to earn PTE directly and are required to stake alongside PTE in our mixed chain pools.

What makes PoolStake unique?
Using Chain Bridge makes moving tokens from one blockchain to another easy. Peet Defi also allows holders to deposit tokens from any of our supported blockchains into a staking pool. Using these applications together enables staking from any supported chain on any other chain, in any pool, for any reward all from our app.
